# Retention_analysis
Review data quality/structure, member behaviour post-employment and evaluate the asset flow to determine how to strategically increase client retention.

The objective of the project is to analyze a dataset related to members leaving their employment and identify post-employment trends that could uncover potential business opportunities for client retention and conversion of group retirement accounts into the Managed program.

The analysis focused on identifying opportunities for the business to preserve assets and continue managing members’ investment accounts through continued engagement and targeted retention strategies.
